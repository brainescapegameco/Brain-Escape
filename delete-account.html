Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Brain Escape account and all associated data at any time. Account deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Option 1: Delete In-App (fastest)

The fastest way is to delete your account from within the Brain Escape app itself:

  1. Open the Brain Escape app and log in
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen
  4. Tap "DELETE ACCOUNT" below the Logout button
  5. Enter your password to confirm
  6. Tap "DELETE FOREVER"

Your account will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, email us and we will delete your account manually:

✉️ EMAIL DELETION REQUEST

Include the following in your email:

  • Email address associated with your account
  • Display name (if you remember it)
  • Confirmation that you own the account

We will delete your account within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your player profile (email, display name, avatar)
  • Your gameplay stats (level, XP, combat record, achievements)
  • Your inventory (cures, potions, weapons, clothing, trophies)
  • Your squad membership (you'll be removed from any squad)
  • All pending squad invitations (sent and received)
  • Your Firebase authentication account
  • Your current location and session data

What May Take Longer

Chat messages you previously sent to other players may remain visible to them for up to 30 minutes (proximity chat) or until their squad is disbanded (squad chat). The messages are not tied to your account after deletion — your display name on old messages will still appear historically.

We do not retain chat messages on our servers long-term.

Can I Recover My Account?

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We do not keep backup copies of deleted accounts. If you want to play again, you'll need to create a new account and start fresh.
