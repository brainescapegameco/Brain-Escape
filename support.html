Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the game asking for my location?
Brain Escape is a GPS-based zombie survival game. Your location places you on the in-game map and determines which zombies, quests, and safe zones are near you. Location is required for the game to function.
Can I play indoors or without moving?
You can play indoors as long as your phone has GPS signal. However, the game is designed around walking — zombies spawn near real-world landmarks, so going outside gives you the best experience. There are no artificial speed restrictions, so you can play from a car or public transit too.
I can't see any zombies on the map
Make sure location permission is granted and your phone has GPS signal. Zombies spawn within 500 meters of your location near real points of interest. If you're in a rural area with few POIs, the game will fall back to random spawn locations. Try reloading the app or moving to an area with more foot traffic.
I became a zombie. How do I get cured?
Find a purple Healer NPC on the map and tap it to accept a waypoint quest. Walk to 3 checkpoints and return to the healer to be cured. Alternatively, another player can use a cure item on you.
How do I avoid getting infected?
Stay at least 50 meters away from zombies. If a zombie gets close, you have a countdown (30 seconds at level 1, shorter at higher levels) to move away. You can also fight the zombie or hide in a Safe Zone (parks, hospitals, cafes) where you are totally immune to infection.
What's the best way to level up?
Combat is the fastest XP. Defeat harder zombies for more XP (easy=30 XP, hard=175 XP, boss=2,500 XP). Accept quests from gold-ringed NPC commanders for huge XP rewards. Unlock achievements for bonus XP — the achievement catalog alone gives over 98,000 XP total.
Can I play with friends?
Yes. Tap any player's name in chat to open their profile and invite them to your squad. Squads can have up to 6 members, share persistent chat, and see each other on the map regardless of distance.
I forgot my password
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" — we'll send a reset link to your email. Check your inbox and spam folder.
How do I delete my account?
Open the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap "DELETE ACCOUNT". Enter your password to confirm. See our full deletion page for details.
Does the game cost anything?
Brain Escape is free to play with no in-app purchases. Everything in the game is earned through play.
Does the game use a lot of data or battery?
The game uses GPS and small amounts of mobile data to sync with other players. Typical usage is similar to navigation apps. To save battery, close the app when not playing — it resets your location and doesn't drain power in the background.
I found a bug / have a suggestion
We'd love to hear it. Email isaacprivett@gmail.com with as much detail as possible — what device, what you were doing, what happened. Screenshots help.
